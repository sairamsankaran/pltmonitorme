*** Won the first prize in ATT Wearable Hackathon held on Feb 8, 2014 ***

We used the Plantronics API for their Concept 1 device to gather accelerometer, gyroscope and pedometer information and developed an app to aid the elderly be monitored for their health and give them emergency access at the tap of a finger.
